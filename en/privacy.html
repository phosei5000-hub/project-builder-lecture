Privacy Policy

Effective: July 21, 2026

Don Quijote Must-Buy Shopping List (the "Site") takes your privacy seriously and follows applicable data protection laws. This policy explains what information the Site collects, how it's used, and what rights you have.

1. Information we collect

The Site has no account or login system, so we don't collect personally identifying information like your name or phone number unless you provide it yourself. The following may be generated automatically or through your own input while using the Site:

  • Automatically collected data: IP address, browser type, device information, visit timestamps, pages viewed, and usage data (for traffic analytics)
  • Shopping list data: The "shopping list" feature stores items only in your browser's local storage (localStorage). It is never sent to a server and the site operator cannot view it. Clearing your browser data removes it.
  • Contact form data: Name (or company), email address, phone number, and message content that you enter yourself when submitting the Contact or Partnership form

2. How we use this information

  • Operating and improving the service, and analyzing traffic to improve content quality
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Preventing abuse and maintaining site security

3. Cookies and advertising

The Site may use cookies for traffic analytics and to serve ads. A cookie is a small piece of data a site's server sends to your browser, sometimes stored on your device's hard drive.

The Site may display ads from third-party advertising partners, including Google. These providers may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this Site and other sites on the internet.

  • Google uses advertising cookies to serve ads based on a user's visits to this Site and other sites on the internet.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of many third-party providers' personalized advertising cookies at www.aboutads.info.
  • Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can change your browser settings to decline cookies. Declining cookies may limit some site functionality.

4. Analytics

The Site may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics to understand usage and improve content. These tools use cookies to collect anonymized traffic statistics that don't identify individuals, and the data they collect is handled under Google's own privacy policy.

5. Data retention

Information collected through inquiries is deleted within a reasonable period after the inquiry is resolved, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your rights

You may ask to review, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Send such requests through the Contact page and we'll act on them promptly.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in law, policy, or the service itself. Any changes will be posted on this page.
